Privacy Policy

Effective June 2, 2026

PackKit helps you pack smart and leave nothing behind. We collect as little as possible and never sell your data. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the control you have.

1. Information we collect

Account details: your name and email address, used to create and secure your account. If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name and email from that provider.

Profile details (all optional): gender, relationship status, pets, and children’s age groups, plus an optional public @handle and bio. Gender is used to hide items meant for another gender (e.g. shaving kit, feminine hygiene) when you view or clone a shared kit; the other details are optional and not currently used to filter. Your @handle and bio appear publicly only on kits you choose to publish. You can skip or change any of these anytime.

Your content: the kits, suitcases, items, quantities, and notes you create.

Limited device data: app version and basic diagnostics needed to keep the app running.

2. How we use your information

To provide the core app — building kits, creating trips, and syncing your checklists.

To tailor which items appear in a kit to your gender (hiding items meant for another gender).

To schedule the on-device reminders you opt into.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

3. Device permissions

Notifications: to schedule local trip reminders. Reminders are created on your device — we don’t send marketing pushes.

Location (optional): to fetch local weather for smarter suggestions. Only requested when relevant.

Calendar (optional): to import upcoming events and pre-fill trip dates. Calendar data stays on your device.

Face ID / Touch ID (optional): used locally to unlock the app. Biometric data never leaves your device and is never sent to us.

4. Service providers

Supabase: hosts our database and handles authentication.

OpenWeatherMap: returns a forecast for a destination you enter; we send only the place and dates, not your identity.

Google / Apple: only if you choose to sign in with them.

These providers process data on our behalf and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes.

5. Data retention & deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Account settings → Delete account.

6. Children

PackKit is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them. Children’s age groups in your profile refer to who you’re packing for — they are not accounts.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. Contact us and we’ll help.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. We’ll revise the effective date above and, for material changes, notify you in-app.

9. Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email us at devtzan@gmail.com.
